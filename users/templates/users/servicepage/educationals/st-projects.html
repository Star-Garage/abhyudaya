Health and safety come first

We earn your trust by keeping your home safe and secure. From following a strict Safety Policy to meeting all industry standards proactively, the experts provide you with the peace of mind you need at every step of the service. At Sarkaar Services, every plumber holds public liability insurance and goes through extensive training, so you are at the right place to book a dependable specialist.

Bringing all tools & equipment

The specialists operate with their own instruments, so we've got you covered there. Plumbing repairs often require the replacement of certain parts, which is yet another thing that the specialists can take care of for you as they can readily supply everything on site (the costs will be added to your final bill).

You only pay for what you get

You save money with a fair hourly-based payment system and plan better with an up-front service estimate. You will pay only for the actual time it took for the specialist to fix your plumbing problem. If a site visit is needed to diagnose a problem and preempt unexpected costs, you might be charged with a minimum call-out fee for the initial hour. After that, you pay on a standard half-hour basis.

Your satisfaction is guaranteed

The performance of every plumber is diligently monitored by supervisors. That’s how we ensure that their work is done at the highest professional level. Apart from the repairs, your qualified expert will gladly give you valuable advice on how to maintain your plumbing in top-notch shape. Our ultimate goal is to bring you complete satisfaction with the services you get. Thus, all plumbing jobs come with full workmanship guarantee for a trouble-free service.

Services Offered

Dripping tap, faucet or leaking pipe? Even if you are not exactly sure what is wrong with your domestic plumbing, the specialist will be able to determine the cause of the problem and fix it in time. From shower replacement and mechanically unblocking drains/toilets to dealing with wet walls and ceilings, we can cover all sorts of plumbing repair services across your home and office. Here are some good examples of common plumbing maintenance duties the specialist can do for you:

  • Electric and gas boiler repair
  • Radiator repair, replacement and installation
  • Fixing variety of plumbing leaks: pipe leaks & bursts, sink basin leaks, etc
  • Detection & repair of indoor and outdoor pipes
  • Fixing dripping or slow running taps, taps/showers that are running cold water only
  • Washing machine and dishwasher installation
  • Shower faults & repairs
  • Drain unblocking services for bathroom/kitchen sinks, toilets, bathtubs, etc.
  • Sump pump replacement
  • Radiator valves changing
  • Toilet repairs and installation
  • Sinks and basins fitting and replacement
  • Fitting replacements
  • Insinkerator or Saniflo repair and replacement
  • Waste disposal installation, removal, replacement and repair
Common Questions

01. I need to make some changes to my booking. How do I do that?

Just log into your Sarkaar account and go to the dashboard section. From there you can reschedule, cancel or add details to your current booking. You can also download the GoSarkaar app to customise every aspect of your service and receive instant, innovative, and convenient ways to manage your booking, or connect to 24/7 friendly customer agents.

02. Can I get a discount on my plumbing service?

You can get a lower price on our whole range of services by joining the Sarkaar Club. If you become a member, you also have the chance to benefit from cool perks like priority bookings and seasonal deals.

03. Is there anything else I need to know?

Prices are inclusive of all taxes. Material supply and delivery costs are added to the service cost, if applicable. If the plumber arrives and is unable to complete the work due to conditions you have failed to provide, there is a call-out fee of 1 hour and the cost of parking/congestion (if applicable).

04. How fast can the technician arrive?

Same-day appointments can be arranged if our schedule allows it. If all slots are currently booked, the professional will make sure to arrive the next day the latest.

05. It’s an emergency! Can the plumber come right now?

We’ll do our best to send the nearby plumber as soon as possible. In the meantime, you can just turn off the main water supply.

06. What about the new materials or fixtures that may be needed?

It's totally lies at the client's discretion. you can get yourself get materials according to your choice or ask the professional to procure the same. The time taken by the professional to get the material will also be included in the working hours.

07. What if no services are availed, how much will I have to pay?

If in case no services are availed, you just need to pay the visiting charges to the professional.

08. Is there any guarantee on the service provided?

Yes, A 30-day guarantee after the service has been delivered is provided on all plumbing services
